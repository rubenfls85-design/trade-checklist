Side Notes (Beginner Definitions)

MA (Moving Average)

Average price over a set number of days. Price above the 50-day MA usually means the short-term trend is up.

RSI

0–100 “heat gauge.” 40–70 is healthy momentum. Above ~80 can mean too hot (pullback risk).

Beta

How fast it moves vs the market. ~1.0 moves like the market. 1.2–2.5 moves enough for short-term trades.
